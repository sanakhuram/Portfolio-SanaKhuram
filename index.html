About Me

I’m a creative professional with a unique background in both art and design. Originally from Pakistan, I’ve been based in Norway for over 12 years. I hold a BFA in Miniature Painting and a Master’s in Interior Design, having worked as an interior architect before shifting my focus to miniature art while raising my children. Now, as my kids have grown, I’ve embraced a new challenge—Front-End Development. Currently studying at Noroff, I’m excited to combine my experience in design and color with the technical skills needed to create engaging, user-friendly digital interfaces.
